FAQs

Frequently Asked questions.

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How quickly can I start with Base360?

You can be up and running in as little as 48 hours. Just connect your listings, and our system handles the rest.

Does Base360 handle maintenance requests?

Yes. Maintenance tasks can be created manually or triggered automatically from issues reported by guests or cleaners, helping you track and resolve them efficiently.

Will Base360 help increase my revenue?

Absolutely. Base360 helps you boost occupancy rates and guest spend through dynamic upsells, automated communication, and real-time performance insights — all while saving hours of manual work.

How does the cleaning dashboard work?

The Cleaning Dashboard automates every step of your cleaning operations. Cleans are automatically created, updated, or removed when bookings change — whether it's a new reservation, cancellation, or extension. Unlike a simple cleaning calendar, it actually manages the process.

Can I connect it with Airbnb or Booking.com?

Yes. Base360 directly connects with major OTAs like Airbnb, Booking.com, and others, syncing reservations, availability, and guest details in real time.

Can I customize my website?

Yes. Base360 lets you fully customize your booking site — from visuals and branding to property layouts and pages — so your website reflects your brand and converts more direct bookings.

Can I customize guest upsells?

Definitely. You can create, edit, and automate custom upsells like airport transfers, early check-ins, or local experiences. Guests see these offers during booking, helping you maximize every stay.

Do you offer support if I get stuck?

Of course. Our support team is always available to assist you with setup, training, or troubleshooting — so you can stay focused on running your business.
